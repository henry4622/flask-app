Delivery

Information

We offer fast and reliable delivery across the majority of the UK. Once your order is confirmed, it will be carefully packed and dispatched within 1–2 working days (for prebuilds usually faster). Most deliveries arrive within 1–2 working days we aim to deliver as fast as possible. You'll receive tracking details as soon as your order is shipped, so you can monitor its progress. We also offer postcode-based eligibility, so please check if we deliver to your area before placing an order. We have teamed up with royal mail and post our items through their services. If you have any questions about delivery times or special requirements, feel free to contact our team. Be sure to check postcode eligibility before placing your order.
